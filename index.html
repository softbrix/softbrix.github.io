The inflation affects the value of your money. Workers will demand more salary and the companies will need to increase their income by adjusting the prices which increases the household total expenses.

This is a simplified way of looking at it since new demands and optimization of current production lines and alterned ways of living will shift how the hoseholds spends their money.

So a simple way to compare the value of your money in history is by converting it to a solid product. The BigMac is a standard global product often produced locally to the market and is therefore a good candidate for comparission.
